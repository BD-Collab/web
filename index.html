Exploring Academic Collaboration Network of Bangladesh

This research work explores the academic collaboration network of Bangladeshi scholers and also shows the current research conditions of Bangladesh, as well as the active network between researchers. It is a 'Data Science' based project. Therefore, the most important part of this work is to collect data. for this research, we collected data from 'Google Scholar' and 'Research Gate'. After the data collection, those data are processed and manipulated to have the desired output. The results has been displayed on a tabular form or as diagram. This Project summarizes the real scenario of the educational sector of Bangladesh.

All the results here are produced based on data from Google Scholar that were collected till October 2020 and from ResearchGate collected till November 2020. Our findings from this research, are the followings :

  • Top 10 Universitys of Google Scholar based on Number of Scholars Profile
  • Top 10 Universities of Google Scholar based on Citation Number
  • Top 10 Universitys of ResearchGate based on Number of Scholars Profile
  • Top 10 Universitys of Research Gate based on Citation Number
  • Top 100 Highly Cited Scholars Profile from Google Scholar
  • Top 100 Research Area from Google Scholar
  • Top 100 Research Area from Research Gate
  • Top 10 Scholar based on highest Index Value from Research Gate
