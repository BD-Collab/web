Exploring Academic Collaboration Network of Bangladesh

This research work explores the academic collaboration network of Bangladeshi scholers and also shows the current research conditions of Bangladesh, as well as the active network between researchers. It is a 'Data Science' based project. Therefore, the most important part of this work is to collect data. for this research, we collected data from 'Google Scholar' and 'ResearchGate'. After the data collection, those data are processed and manipulated to have the desired output. The results has been displayed on a tabular form and as diagram. This Project summarizes the real scenario of the educational sector of Bangladesh.

All the results here are produced based on data from Google Scholar that were collected till October 2020 and from ResearchGate collected till November 2020. Our findings from this research, are the followings :

  • Top 10 Universities with Highest Number of Research Profiles from Google Scholar
  • Top 10 Universities with Highest Number of Research Profiles from ResearchGate
  • Top 10 University with Highest Number of Citations (Google Scholar)
  • Top 10 Researchers with Highest Number of Index Value (ResearchGate)
  • Top 10 Highly Cited Researchers in Bangladesh (Google Scholar)
  • Top Scholars Based on Citation Number (Google Scholar)
  • Research Areas (Google Scholar)
  • Research Areas (ResearchGate)
